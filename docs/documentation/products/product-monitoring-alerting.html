Monitoring and alerting guide

List of grafana dashboards and alerting channels.

Prerequisites

To view the grafana dashboards below, you will need the following.

  • @justice.gov.uk account.
  • Be a member of the MoJO-EntApp-Grafana_Viewer AzureAD group.
  • Slack access which is available to any @justice.gov.uk or @digital.justice.gov.uk account. Request via support portal
