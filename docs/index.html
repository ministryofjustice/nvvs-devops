Network, Voice and Video Service DevOps (NVVS DevOps)

The NVVS DevOps team sit within the Network Services area. Our Team is made up of Developers, Product Managers and Operations Managers. Please see Our Products to find a list of applications we are currently responsible for.

Who is this for?

This documentation is for anyone interested in the NVVS DevOps team and its core values.

Our Products

Product Description
DNS Public name resolution for staff devices when local and remote. This service protects user traffic by forwarding queries to Protective DNS.
DHCP Automatic device configuration of MoJO DNS. This service helps devices when connected at sites reach the internet.
IMAP Our Infrastructure Monitoring and Alerting Platform allows us to monitor the health of our infrastructure and products.
NACS The Network Access Control service authenticates access to the network at a device level.
Log Shipping This is the Log Shipping infrastructure used by the Ministry of Justice to forward logs to the Operational Security Team.
PKI Public Key Infrastructure for devices and users. This repository can redeploy the EC2 instances which Entrust build their managed service on.
SMTP Relay The Simple Mail Transort Protocol Relay service allows legacy MoJ applications and services to send unauthenticated email to staff devices and brings this system in house.
